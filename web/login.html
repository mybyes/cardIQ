About us

Built for the points-rich, time-poor.

CardIQ started with a simple frustration: Indians earn lakhs of reward points every year, then redeem them for a fraction of their worth — a ₹0.20 statement credit instead of a ₹0.80 business-class seat. We built the intelligence layer that closes that gap, so every point you earn actually takes you somewhere.

Independent

We don't take deposits or issue cards. Our only job is to get you the most value per point — never to push a product.

India-first

Built around Indian cards, programs and transfer partners — HDFC, Axis, Amex, SBI and the airlines you actually fly.

Private by design

No bank logins, no card numbers, no inbox scraping. You stay in control of your data, always — aligned with India's DPDP Act.

Good to know

Questions, answered.

Is it really free?

Yes — building your wallet and seeing your points' ₹ value is free. We may earn a referral fee if you choose to apply for a card we suggest; that never changes the advice you see.

Do you need my bank login or card numbers?

Never. You tell us which cards you hold — we never ask for card numbers, CVVs or net-banking credentials. Spend tracking is optional, via forwarded statement emails or a pasted CSV.

How accurate is the points valuation?

We value points by the best realistic redemption path — researched transfer ratios and award sweet spots — refreshed regularly and stamped with a data date. We show best / typical / conservative so you can judge for yourself.

Do you sell my data?

No. We don't sell or share your personal data. CardIQ is built to comply with India's DPDP Act, and you can delete your wallet at any time.

Which cards and programs are supported?

Major Indian rewards cards (HDFC, Axis, Amex, SBI and more) and 16+ airline & hotel transfer partners — and the list keeps growing.
